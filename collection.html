Our wide variety of tea

Tea is a globally consumed beverage with a rich history spanning centuries and diverse cultural traditions. Derived from the leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant, the main types of tea - green, black, oolong, white, and pu-erh - undergo different levels of oxidation and processing, resulting in a spectrum of flavors, aromas, and appearances. Additionally, herbal teas, such as chamomile and peppermint, are popular alternatives made from various plants.

The following are the types of tea found at Chai Kafi and their benefits:-

  • Green Tea: Known for its fresh and grassy flavor. Unoxidized leaves retain their natural green color. Rich in antioxidants and believed to have various health benefits.
  • Black Tea: Fully oxidized tea leaves result in a dark color and robust flavor. Popular varieties include Assam, Darjeeling, and Earl Grey. Contains caffeine and is often served with milk and sugar.
  • Oolong Tea: Partially oxidized tea, falling between green and black tea. Exhibits a wide range of flavors, from floral and fruity to smoky and earthy. Popular varieties include Tie Guan Yin and Da Hong Pao.
  • White Tea: Minimal processing, made from young tea leaves and buds. Delicate flavor with subtle sweetness. Contains less caffeine compared to green and black tea.
  • Herbal Tea: Not technically tea as it is made from herbs, fruits, flowers, and spices. Wide variety, including chamomile, peppermint, and hibiscus. Often caffeine-free and known for various health benefits.
  • Chai Tea: A spiced tea blend, typically made with black tea, spices, and milk. Common spices include cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, and cloves. Originating from India, chai has become popular worldwide.
